Recipe Of The Day: Spegetthi Carbnara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese and pepper. Its simple, delicious and perfect for any occasions

Ingredients

  • 200g Spegetthi
  • 100g pancella
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pacorino cheese
  • 50g Paemesan
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps To Prepare

  1. Boil the Spegetthiin salted water until a dente.
  2. Fry the pancetla until crispy
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with the grated cheese
  4. Drain the Spegetthi and mix with pancetla
  5. Remove the lead and str in eggs and cheese mixture
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately
  7. Salt
